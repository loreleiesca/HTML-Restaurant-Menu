Origin Story

It all started on Spring Mountain, Ron, the founder and owner of H-Thai-ML established this location with his mother. Born and raised in the area, they had a dream to start their own restuarant using recipes from Ron's great-great grandmother that had been passed down to his mother, and now her to him.
